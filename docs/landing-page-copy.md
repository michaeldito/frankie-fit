# Frankie Fit Landing Page Copy

## Purpose

This document captures the working landing page copy for Frankie Fit version one. It is meant to express the product clearly, align with Frankie's voice, and give the project a strong outward-facing story.

This is marketing copy, which means it focuses on how the product is presented to prospective users. It should work alongside the product brief, voice decisions, and in-app copy.

## Landing Page Goal

The landing page should help a new visitor understand:

- what Frankie Fit is
- who it is for
- why it is different
- why it matters
- what action to take next

For version one, the likely primary action is:

- get early access

Secondary actions can include:

- see how it works
- meet Frankie

## Audience

Primary audience for this page:

- younger adults who already care about fitness and want to level up without tedious tracking

## Core Positioning

Frankie Fit is not just a logging app. It is an AI-native wellness coach that helps users stay consistent across activity, diet, and mental wellness through one simple conversation-centered experience.

## Section: Problem

### Headline

> Most health apps ask too much and help too little.

### Body Copy

> If you have ever tried to get more consistent with your health, you probably know the pattern. You start motivated, download an app, and then spend more time logging your life than actually living it.

> Frankie Fit is built to change that. Instead of filling out endless forms, you talk to Frankie. The system handles the heavy lifting behind the scenes so you can focus on feeling better and staying consistent.

## Section: The Three Pillars

### Headline

> Built around the parts of health that actually connect.

### Intro Copy

> Health is not one thing. Frankie Fit brings the most important pieces together so your guidance reflects your real life, not isolated data points.

### Pillar 1

Title:

> Exercise

Body:

### Pillar 2

Title:

> Diet

Body:

### Pillar 3

Title:

> Wellness

Body:

## Section: Meet Frankie

### Headline

> Meet Frankie

### Body Copy

> Frankie is the coach at the center of the experience. Calm, thoughtful, and grounded, Frankie helps you stay honest about where you are, clear about where you want to go, and practical about what to do next.

> Not a drill sergeant. Not a spreadsheet. Not a guilt machine. Just a steady coach in your corner.

## Section: Privacy and Trust

### Headline

> Your health data should be treated with care.

### Body Copy

> Frankie Fit is designed with privacy in mind. Real user insights should be handled with strong boundaries, and product learning should default to aggregate, privacy-conscious views wherever possible.

> Frankie Fit also makes a clear distinction between wellness coaching and medical care. The product is here to support better habits, reflection, and consistency, not replace licensed professionals.

## Founder-Friendly Messaging Notes

- lead with reduced friction
- reinforce that the product is more than logging
- position Frankie as a calm coach, not a gimmick
- avoid overpromising medical or clinical expertise
- emphasize consistency over perfection
